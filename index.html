Quiz Hub

Quiz Hub is a concept and the brain child and baby of Virtual Quiz Host Jay Flynn. His aim is to make it easier for people to find everything they might need for their next quiz

Jay's aim for Quiz Hub is to make it easier for people to create and host their own quizzes, or even have someone do it all for them! When we open the doors completely, you will find:

  • Access to Jay's library of over 25k quiz questions, some will be available completely free, others available in different ways
  • A quiz directory, were you can find a quiz wherever you are, from regular weekly quizzes to one off specials
  • A forum to meet like minded people interested in quizzes, to share ideas ore debate that tricky question in last nights quiz! in

And a lot more secret features to be added as we launch!
